What is stem cell therapy?

Stem cell therapy treats physical pain and injury by adding natural cells that impact regeneration and healing. Once injected into the area of concern, the stem cells promote the natural regeneration process and may regenerate different types of tissue.

What is platelet rich plasma therapy?

Platelet rich plasma therapy treats physical pain and injury by increasing growth factors and decreasing inflammation. Once injected into the area of concern, the growth factors and chemicals will speed up the natural healing process. PRP can also be used in conjunction with stem cell therapy.

Stem cell therapy is a versatile treatment that can be beneficial for a wide variety of injuries and degeneration. Common issues treated with stem cell therapy are large joint and spine arthritis, ligament/tendon tears, meniscus tears, inflammation, and more.

What types of stem cell injections do we offer?

We offer stem cell injections from three sources - bone marrow, fat tissue, and amniotic fluid. The bone marrow and fat tissue stem cells are taken from the patient, processed, and injected into the area of concern. Amniotic fluid stem cells are taken from placental tissue. The placental tissue is donated by pre-approved, thoroughly screened individuals undergoing scheduled C-section births. The mother and child are not harmed during this process.
